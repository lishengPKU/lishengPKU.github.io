Li Sheng（李胜）

Sheng Li（李胜） received his Ph.D. in 2005 from the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Science (CAS) Currently, he is a Professor of the School of Computer Sciences, Peking University He works as a member of the Graphics and Interaction Lab. and publishes over 40 refereed papers in prestigious international conferences and journals. He applied 50 Chinese / USA patents, 28 are authorized. He is a member of ACM and IEEE, Member of Technical Committee Virtual Reality and Visualization Technology, CCF, Member of Technical Committee Computer Graphics and CAD, CCF, China.
